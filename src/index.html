A museum-grade laboratory of color science — every concept manipulated, never narrated.

The encyclopedia is composed of 518 independent interactive modules across 12 domains: physical optics, visual physiology, colourant chemistry, colorimetry, design theory, display engineering, print reproduction, anthropology, biology, imaging, computation, and cognition. What follows is the first cohort of golden modules — the visual and editorial baseline against which every successor will be measured.
